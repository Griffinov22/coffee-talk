Coffee Talk

Easily Explained Cafe drinks!

Espresso

^

Espresso is made by pressing steaming hot water through finely grinded coffee. Ideally, you shoot for a 2:1 ratio of coffee to water for espresso. Espresso is the base for many drinks inlcuding the latte.

Latte

^

A latte is a combination of any milk and espresso served in any type of cup. Depending on the size of the drink, the latte might get more or less 'shots' of espresso. A standard 12-ounce latte should get around two shots of espresso. After the shots are pulled, the barista will steam his/her milk and pour on top of the espresso.

Espresso

^

Espresso is made by pressing steaming hot water through finely grinded coffee. Ideally, you shoot for a 2:1 ratio of coffee to water for espresso. The typical weight measurement is in grams. Espresso is the base for many drinks inlcuding the latte. Caffeine content is for espresso is not a one size fits all. Blonde roasts typically have the most caffeine per coffee bean for all varieties.

Latte

^

A latte is a combination of any milk and espresso served in any type of cup. Depending on the size of the drink, the latte might get more or less 'shots' of espresso. A standard 12-ounce latte should get around two shots of espresso. After the shots are pulled, the barista will steam his/her milk and pour on top of the espresso.
